How can you help?

Simple! All you have to do is type in any question that you would normally ask a chatbot in the field below and send. Yep ANY question..Well it would be great if your question is related to one of the following categories (althought it's not compulsory):

  • Mood (eg. How are you?)
  • Origin (eg. Where are you from?)
  • Identity (eg. Who are you?)
  • Negative (eg. You are dumb)
  • Greeting (eg. Hello!)
  • Goodbye (eg. Bye!)
  • Thank (eg. Thank you!)
  • Ability (eg. What can you do?)

You can also help me out (if you want) by labelling your questions for me.

Ask as many questions as you want. The more questions we get, the better!
