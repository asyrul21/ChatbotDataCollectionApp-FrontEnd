How can you help?

Simple! All you have to do is type in any question that you would normally ask a chatbot in the field above and send. Yep ANY question..Well it would be great if your question is related to one of the following categories (althought it's not compulsory):

  • Mood (eg. How are you?)
  • Origin (eg. Where are you from?)
  • Identity (eg. Who are you?)
  • Negative (eg. You are dumb)
  • Greeting (eg. Hello!)
  • Goodbye (eg. Bye!)
  • Thank (eg. Thank you!)
  • Ability (eg. What can you do?)
  • Meaning of Life (eg. What is the meaning of life?)
  • Age (eg. How old are you?)
  • Positive (eg. Nice to meet you)
  • Create workflow (eg. Help me create a workflow)

You can also help me out (if you want) by labelling your questions for me.
